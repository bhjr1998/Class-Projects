An introduction...

This site is created to give you knowledge about the heavy class in Team Fortress 2.

Here is a quick rundown of all the pages:

  • Maps is about the different maps that are used in competitive and gives a general overview on how to play them.
  • Weapons goes over the various weapons that the heavy has.
  • Playstyles gives a brief summary of the three general playstyles for heavy.
  • Advanced Tactics will talk about various tricks and quirks about heavy.
  • Contact is the page where you can go to give feedback whether on the information I have given or just on the website itself.
